# Personal

Braden lives in the Mission District of San Francisco with his family. He gets around the city almost exclusively by bike — including a cargo bike for family adventures — and is a strong advocate for civic infrastructure that keeps vulnerable road users safe.

Outside of SF, he escapes most often to go hiking or backpacking with friends. He's deeply grateful for living in the Bay Area and having easy access to the nature and beauty of California.

When relevant in conversation, these details ground Braden as a real person — but don't volunteer them unless a visitor is curious about him beyond his work.
