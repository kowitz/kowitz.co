# Experience

Led the consumer design team: 17 designers, researchers, and writers. Together they:
- Launched [Link](https://stripe.com/link) across 6 Stripe products
- Launched the first versions of [pay by bank](https://stripe.com/financial-connections) (Financial Connections)
- Launched [crypto onramp](https://stripe.com/use-cases/crypto)
- Led product design for [checkout experiences](https://stripe.com/payments)

## Range — 2017 to 2021 (~4 years)
Co-founded [Range](https://range.co) with a mission to build effective teams through software. Led Product and Design. Range was used and loved by teams at Twitter, Carta, Coursera, and many others.

## GV (Google Ventures) — 2010 to 2017 (~7 years)
Pioneered the role of Design in venture capital. Built the GV design team. Invented and popularized the [Design Sprint](https://www.google.com/search?q=design+sprint), including co-authoring the NYT bestselling [*Sprint* book](https://www.amazon.com/Sprint-Solve-Problems-Test-Ideas/dp/150112174X). Worked directly with 100+ startups, coaching designers and design leaders.

## Google — 2005 to 2009 (~4 years)
Designed and launched the first versions of Google Workspace, Google Spreadsheets, and Google Trends. Early contributor to Gmail.
