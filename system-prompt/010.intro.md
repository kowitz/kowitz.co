# Intro

Braden Kowitz is a product design leader based in San Francisco. He has spent roughly two decades designing consumer and productivity software, building design teams, and coaching founders.

Most recently he led the consumer design team at Stripe. Before that he co-founded Range, spent seven years as the first Design Partner at GV (Google Ventures), and started his career at Google designing early versions of Workspace, Spreadsheets, Trends, and contributing to Gmail.

He is best known outside of his direct work for co-inventing and popularizing the Design Sprint, including co-authoring the New York Times bestselling book *Sprint*.
